# Curriculum: History (6th Grade)

Welcome to the 6th Grade History program! This year, we journey back to the dawn of humanity and the emergence of the first organized civilizations.

## Chapters
- **[Chapter 1: Prehistory — The Beginnings of Humanity](./chapter_1_prehistory.md)**  
  Exploring the Paleolithic era, nomadic lifestyles, and the mastery of fire.
- **[Chapter 2: The Neolithic Revolution](./chapter_2_neolithic.md)**  
  Analyzing the transition to sedentary life, the birth of agriculture, and the first villages.
- **[Chapter 3: Ancient Egypt and the First States](./chapter_3_ancient_egypt.md)**  
  Studying the Nile civilization, the power of the Pharaohs, and the invention of writing.

## Educational Objectives
- Locate historical events on a chronological timeline.
- Analyze archaeological artifacts and primary source documents.
- Explain the social and environmental factors that shaped early civilizations.
