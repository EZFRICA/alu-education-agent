# Chapter 3 — Ancient Egypt and the First States (6th Grade)

## Objective
Discover the organization of one of the first great civilizations.

## Key Notion to Remember
Ancient Egypt developed along the **Nile River**. The King of Egypt was called the **Pharaoh**.

Key elements:
- **The Nile**: The "gift" of Egypt (floods provided fertile soil).
- **Pharaoh**: An absolute ruler considered a living god.
- **Hieroglyphs**: A complex writing system using symbols.
- **Pyramids**: Massive tombs for the Pharaohs.

## Simple Explanation
Egypt was like a giant kingdom where the Nile was the road and the source of life. The Pharaoh was the boss of everyone, and he built giant pyramids so people would remember him forever.

## Summary
- Egypt is a gift of the Nile.
- Pharaoh had absolute power.
- Invention of writing (Hieroglyphs) allowed the first states to manage their people.
