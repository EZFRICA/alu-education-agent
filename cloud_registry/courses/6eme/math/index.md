# Curriculum: Mathematics (6th Grade)

Welcome to the 6th Grade Mathematics program! This year, we establish the foundations of numbers, operations, and geometry.

## Chapters
- **[Chapter 1: Simple Fractions](./chapter_1_simple_fractions.md)**  
  Introduction to parts of a whole.
- **[Chapter 2: Decimal Numbers](./chapter_2_decimal_numbers.md)**  
  Mastering place value and calculations with points.
- **[Chapter 3: Angles and Geometry](./chapter_3_angles_and_geometry.md)**  
  Measuring the world with degrees and shapes.

## Objectives
- Perform accurate calculations with whole and decimal numbers.
- Understand and represent fractions visually.
- Use geometric tools like the ruler and protractor.
