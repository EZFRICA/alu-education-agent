# Chapter 3 — Angles and Geometry (6th Grade)

## Objective
Identify different types of angles and learn how to use a protractor.

## Key Notion to Remember
An **angle** is the space between two intersecting lines. It is measured in **degrees (°)**.

Types of angles:
1. **Acute angle**: Less than 90°.
2. **Right angle**: Exactly 90° (like the corner of a square).
3. **Obtuse angle**: Between 90° and 180°.
4. **Straight angle**: Exactly 180° (a flat line).

## Simple Explanation
Think of a clock:
- At 3:00, the hands make a **Right angle**.
- At 2:00, they make an **Acute angle**.
- At 6:00, they make a **Straight angle**.

## Summary
- Use a protractor to measure angles accurately.
- The vertex is the point where the two lines meet.
- Practice recognizing angles in objects around you.
