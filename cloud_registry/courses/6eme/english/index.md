# Curriculum: English (6th Grade)

Welcome to the 6th Grade English program! This year, we focus on building strong reading habits and understanding the foundations of a story.

## Chapters
- **[Chapter 1: Reading Comprehension](./chapter_1_reading_comprehension.md)**  
  Master the Who, Where, and What of any story.

## Objectives
- Develop active reading skills.
- Identify characters and settings in a narrative.
- Summarize texts in your own words.
