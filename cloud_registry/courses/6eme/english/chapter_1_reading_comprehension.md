# Chapter 1 — Reading Comprehension (6th Grade)

## Objective
Read a short text and identify the core elements:
- Who is involved?
- Where is it happening?
- What is occurring?

## Key Notion to Remember
To understand a story, always answer these 3 questions:
1. **Who?** (The characters)
2. **Where?** (The setting/location)
3. **What?** (The action or plot)

## Simple Explanation
Read the text twice:
1. **First reading**: Get the "big picture" of the story.
2. **Second reading**: Highlight the important details (names, places, main events).

## Example
Sentence: *"In the morning, Nora arrives at school with her blue backpack."*
- **Who?** Nora
- **Where?** At school
- **What?** She arrives

## Summary
- Reading twice helps a lot.
- Look for Who / Where / What.
- Try to retell the story in your own words to check your understanding.
