# Curriculum: History (5th Grade)

Welcome to the 5th Grade History program! This year, we examine the structural organization of medieval society through its power dynamics, faith, and economic expansion.

## Chapters
- **[Chapter 1: The Manorial System](./chapter_1_lordship.md)**  
  Analyzing the relationship between Lords and peasants, the organization of the demesne, and the tenures.
- **[Chapter 2: The Church and Society](./chapter_2_church_and_society.md)**  
  Evaluating the cultural and political influence of the Catholic Church in the Middle Ages.
- **[Chapter 3: Cities and Trade](./chapter_3_cities_and_trade.md)**  
  Discovering the rebirth of urban centers, the development of trade fairs, and the rise of the bourgeoisie.

## Educational Objectives
- Differentiate between the three orders of medieval society.
- Analyze medieval iconography and architecture (cathedrals, castles).
- Explain historical evolution through the analysis of causes and consequences.
