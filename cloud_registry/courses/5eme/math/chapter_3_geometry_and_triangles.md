# Chapter 3 — Geometry and Triangles (5th Grade)

## Objective
Learn the properties of triangles and how to construct them.

## Key Notion to Remember
- **Sum of Angles**: In any triangle, the sum of the three angles is always **180°**.
- **Triangle Inequality**: The sum of the lengths of two sides must always be greater than the length of the third side.

## Special Triangles
1. **Isosceles Triangle**: 2 equal sides and 2 equal angles.
2. **Equilateral Triangle**: 3 equal sides and 3 angles of 60°.
3. **Right Triangle**: 1 right angle (90°).

## Simple Explanation
If you have three sticks of 10cm, 2cm, and 2cm, you **cannot** make a triangle! (The 10cm side is too long for the others to meet).

## Summary
- Angles total = 180°.
- You can't build a triangle with just any side lengths.
- Every special triangle has unique properties to memorize.
