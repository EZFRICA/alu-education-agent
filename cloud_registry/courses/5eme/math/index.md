# Curriculum: Mathematics (5th Grade)

Welcome to the 5th Grade Mathematics program! This curriculum focuses on expanding numerical understanding to relative numbers and mastering complex ratios and geometry.

## Chapters
- **[Chapter 1: Relative Numbers](./chapter_1_relative_numbers.md)**  
  Introduction to positive and negative integers, comparisons on number lines, and real-world applications.
- **[Chapter 2: Fractions and Proportionality](./chapter_2_fractions_and_proportionality.md)**  
  Mastering ratio simplification, cross-multiplication methods, and identifying proportional relationships.
- **[Chapter 3: Geometry and Triangles](./chapter_3_geometry_and_triangles.md)**  
  Exploring triangle properties, the sum of angles theorem, and construction techniques for special triangles.

## Educational Objectives
- Perform mental and written calculations with relative numbers.
- Solve complex proportionality problems in various contexts (scales, percentages).
- Demonstrate geometric theorems through precise construction.
