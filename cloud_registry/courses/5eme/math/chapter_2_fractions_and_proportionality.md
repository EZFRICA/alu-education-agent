# Chapter 2 — Fractions and Proportionality (5th Grade)

## Objective
Learn how to simplify fractions and recognize proportional situations.

## Key Notion to Remember
- **Equality of Fractions**: You can multiply or divide the numerator and denominator by the same number without changing the fraction.
- **Proportionality**: Two quantities are proportional if you can get one from the other by multiplying by a constant number (the coefficient).

## Simple Explanation
**Fractions**: 2/4 is the same as 1/2 because if you divide both 2 and 4 by 2, you get 1/2.
**Proportionality**: If 1 kg of apples costs $2, then 3 kg will cost $6 ($2 x 3). The coefficient is 2.

## Method: Cross-Multiplication
To find a missing value in a proportional table:
Multiply the diagonal numbers and divide by the third one.
(A x D) / B = C

## Summary
- A fraction can be simplified.
- Proportionality is a constant relationship between two things.
- We use proportionality for recipes, scales, and prices.
