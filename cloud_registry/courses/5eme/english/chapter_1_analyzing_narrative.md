# Chapter 1 — Analyzing a Narrative (5th Grade)

## Objective
Identify the key components of a story to understand its meaning and structure.

## Key Notion to Remember
A narrative is built using the **Narrative Arc**:
1. **Initial Situation**: The beginning (Who? Where? When?). Everything is calm.
2. **Inciting Incident**: An event that disrupts the calm and starts the action.
3. **Rising Action**: The different adventures or challenges faced by the hero.
4. **Resolution**: The moment the main problem is solved.
5. **Final Situation**: The end of the story, a new state of calm.

## Summary
- A story always has a beginning, a middle (with a problem), and an end.
- Identifying the Narrative Arc helps you understand the author's logic.
