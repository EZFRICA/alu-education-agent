# Chapter 2 — Grammatical Functions (5th Grade)

## Objective
Distinguish between the **nature** (what a word is) and the **function** (what a word does in a sentence).

## Key Notion to Remember
- **Nature**: The "identity card" of the word (Noun, Verb, Adjective).
- **Function**: The "job" the word is doing (Subject, Object, Complement).

## Summary
- Nature = What it is.
- Function = What it does.
